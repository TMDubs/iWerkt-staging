Sign Up

LET'S GET THINGS STARTED.

  • HOW DO I SIGN UP?

  • It's easy! Just install the app onto your phone (or other mobile device) via the AppStore for iPhone, or GooglePlay for Android users. Then click SIGNUP! We just need your name, email address, agency, a password, and a photo.

  • WHAT IS MY USERNAME?

  • Your username is the email address you used to sign up.

  • WHAT IF I FORGOT MY PASSWORD? Gaaaahhhh!

  • No problem! Click on the Question Mark (?) on the LOGIN screen, then enter your email address. We'll send you your password ASAP.

  • DO I HAVE TO ADD A PHOTO TO COMPLETE THE SIGNUP PROCESS?

  • With models as our users, we thought it was very likely that they have beautiful photos. Your photo becomes the backdrop for your homescreen. You are not required to choose a photo for this, but if you leave it blank you'll only see a gray background - and that seems just so, so sad...
